About Company

Your Chef Staffing Solutions

Our mission at Grace Recruitment is to meticulously match and prepare chefs to surpass our clients' culinary expectations, cultivating a team of passionate and outstanding culinary professionals. We are devoted to fostering an environment where our chefs can thrive and excel in their culinary careers.


We are committed to elevating the dining experiences in Ireland's restaurants and hotels through exceptional culinary talent and high-quality food services. As we expand, our focus will remain on linking our clients with the premier chefs, while continually offering significant opportunities for our skilled culinary team members.
